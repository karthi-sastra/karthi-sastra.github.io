About Me

Professor & Researcher in Medicinal Chemistry

I am a dedicated professor and researcher with over 15 years of experience in computational drug design and medicinal chemistry. My research focuses on developing novel therapeutic agents for cardiovascular diseases and antimicrobial resistance using cutting-edge computational approaches.

My work integrates molecular modeling, cheminformatics, and structural bioinformatics to identify and optimize potential drug candidates. I have successfully led multiple research projects resulting in high-impact publications and patent applications.

In addition to my research, I am passionate about teaching and mentoring the next generation of scientists in drug design, medicinal chemistry, and bioinformatics.
